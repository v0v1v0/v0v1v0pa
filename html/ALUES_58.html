COCOASoil R Documentation

Cocoa soil requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the soil characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Cocoa.

Format

A data frame with 10 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • CFragm - Coarse fragment (Vol.%)

  • SoilDpt - Soil depth (cm)

  • CaCO3 - CaCO3 (%)

  • Gyps - Gypsum (%)

  • CECc - Apparent CEC Clay (cmol (+)/kg clay)

  • BS - Base Saturation (%)

  • SumBCs - Sum of basic caions (cmol (+)/kg soil)

  • pHH2O - pH H2O

  • OC - Organic carbon (%)

  • ECedS - ECe (dS/m)

See Also
