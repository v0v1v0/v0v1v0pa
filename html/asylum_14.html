awaiting_decision R Documentation

Asylum applications awaiting a decision

Description

Asylum applications awaiting an initial decision or further review, by nationality and applicant type.

Usage

awaiting_decision

Format

A data frame with 32,695 rows and 7 variables:

Date

Date

Nationality

Nationality of people awaiting a decision

Region

World region the nation is in

Applicant type

'Main applicant' or 'Dependant'

Application stage

'Pending initial decision' or 'Pending further review'

Duration

'More than 6 months', '6 months or less', or 'N/A - Further review'

Applications

Number of applications

...

Source

https://www.gov.uk/government/statistical-data-sets/asylum-and-resettlement-datasets
