oral_hearings_volume R Documentation

Volume of oral hearings represented by the Home Office

Description

The volume of oral hearings represented by the Home Office divided by the total number of oral hearing.

Usage

oral_hearings_volume

Format

A data frame with 13 rows and 4 variables:

Financial Year

Financial year

Asylum appeals represented at hearing (%)

Percent of sylum appeals represented at hearing

Asylum Oral Hearings

Number of asylum oral hearings

Asylum Oral Hearings Represented by HO

Number of asylum oral hearings represented by the Home Office

...
