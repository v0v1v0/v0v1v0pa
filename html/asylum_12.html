asylum_productivity_breakdown R Documentation

Breakdown of asylum productivity

Description

Breakdown of asylum productivity by month.

Usage

asylum_productivity_breakdown

Format

A data frame with 45 rows and 6 variables:

Date

Date

Initial Decisions

Number of initial decisions made

Substantive Interviews

Number of substantive interviews completed

Total Principal Stages Completed

Total number of initial decisions and substantive interviews

Asylum Caseworking Staff

Number of asylum caseworking staff

Productivity

Productivity of staff (total principal stages completed divided by asylum caseworking staff)

...

Source

https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/migration-transparency-data#uk-visas-and-immigration
