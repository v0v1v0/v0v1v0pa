GodwitArrival R Documentation

Godwit Arrival Dates

Description

Arrival dates for males and females in 10 pairs of Black-tailed Godwits (Limosa limosa)

Format

A data frame with 10 observations on the following 2 variables.

female

a numeric vector

male

a numeric vector
