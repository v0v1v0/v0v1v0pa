appeals_determined R Documentation

Asylum appeals determined

Description

Outcomes of asylum appeals raised at the First-Tier Tribunal, by nationality and sex.

Usage

appeals_determined

Format

A data frame with 13,426 rows and 8 variables:

Date

Date

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Nationality

Nationality of people applying

Region

World region the nation is in

Outcome

'Allowed', 'Dismissed', or 'Withdrawn'

Sex

Gender/sex of applicants

Appeals determined

Number of appeals determined

...

Source

https://www.gov.uk/government/statistical-data-sets/asylum-and-resettlement-datasets
