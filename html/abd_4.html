AlgaeCO2 R Documentation

Carbon Dioxide and Growth Rate in Algae

Description

Growth rates of the unicellular alga Chlamydomonas after 1,000 generations of selection under High and Normal levels of carbon dioxide.

Format

A data frame with 14 observations on the following 2 variables.

treatment

a factor with levels Normal and High

growthrate

a numeric vector

Source

Collins, S. and G. Bell. 2004. Phenotypic consequences of 1,000 generations of selection at elevated CO_{2} in a green alga. Nature 431: 566-569.

Examples

AlgaeCO2
xyplot(growthrate ~ treatment, AlgaeCO2, type = c('p', 'a'))
