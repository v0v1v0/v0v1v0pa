COWPEAWater R Documentation

Cowpea water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Cowpea.

Format

A data frame with 6 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WgAv - Precipitation of growing cycle (mm)

  • WmAv1 - Mean precipitation of first month (mm)

  • WmAv2 - Mean precipitation of second month (mm)

  • WmAv3 - Mean precipitation of third month (mm)

  • WmAv4 - Mean precipitation of fourth month (mm)

  • WmhAv4 - Relative humidity at harvest stage (%)
