SO2 levels from the LA pollution study

Description

Sulfur dioxide levels from the LA pollution study

Format

The format is: Time-Series [1:508] from 1970 to 1980: 3.37 2.59 3.29 3.04 3.39 2.57 2.35 3.38 1.5 2.56 ...
