dublin_regulation R Documentation

Dublin regulation

Description

Transfers and requests for transfer under the Dublin regulation, by EU member state and article.

Usage

dublin_regulation

Format

A data frame with 3,508 rows and 8 variables:

Date

The period in which the request or transfer of an asylum applicant under the Dublin Regulation was made

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Member State

As applicable, the EU Member State which either raises, or receives, a request or transfer of an asylum applicant under the Dublin Regulation

In / Out

The direction of a request or transfer: either into, or out of, the UK

Request / Transfer

Whether the case is a request for transfer, or a transfer, of an asylum applicant

Article

The Article (rule) of the Dublin Regulation under which the request or transfer is carried out

Count

The number of transfers, or requests to transfer under the Dublin Regulation

...

Source
