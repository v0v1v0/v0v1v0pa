BARLEYTemp R Documentation

Barley temp requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the temp characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Barley.

Format

A data frame with 3 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • TmAv2 - Mean temp. crop development stage (2nd month) (°C)

  • TmAv3 - Mean temp. of the flowering stage (°C)

  • TmAv4 - Mean temp. of the ripening stage (°C)

See Also
