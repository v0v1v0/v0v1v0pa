PINEAPPLEWater R Documentation

Pineapple water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Pineapple.

Format

A data frame with 2 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WyAv - Annual precipitation (mm)

  • WyhAv - Mean annual rel. humidity (%)

See Also

  • Yen, B. T., Pheng, K. S., and Hoanh, C. T. (2006). LUSET: Land Use Suitability Evaluation Tool User's Guide. International Rice Research Institute.
