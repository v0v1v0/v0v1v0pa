local_authority_support R Documentation

Section 95 support by local authority

Description

Asylum seekers in receipt of section 95 support by local authority.

Usage

local_authority_support

Format

A data frame with 18,568 rows and 9 variables:

Date

The date at which the number of people in receipt of support refers to

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Support Type

Section 95 or Section 4 support?

UK Region

The UK region of the individual's registered address

Local Authority

The Local Authority of the individual's registered address

LAD Code

Local Authority (district) code as supplied by ONS

Accommodation Type

Whether the support provided is subsistence only, or includes provision of accommodation

People

Number of people in receipt of section 95 support

...
