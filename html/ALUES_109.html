MarinduqueLT R Documentation

Land and Terrain Chracteristics of Marinduque, Philippines

Description

A dataset containing the land and terrain characteristics of the land units in Marinduque, Philippines.

Format

A data frame with 881 rows and 6 columns

Details

  • Lat - Latitude of Land Units;

  • Lon - Longitude of Land Units;

  • CECc - Apparent CEC Clay (cmol (+)/kg clay);

  • pHH20 - pH H2O;

  • CFragm - Coarse fragment (Vol.%);

  • SoilTe - 12 classes of soil texture (Soil Taxonomy);
