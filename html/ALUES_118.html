OILPALMTerrain R Documentation

Oil Palm terrain requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the terrain characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Oil Palm.

Format

A data frame with 4 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • Slope - Slope

  • Flood - Flooding

  • Drainage4 - Drainage (4) Medium & fine textured soils

  • Drainage5 - Drainage (5) Coarse textured soils

See Also
