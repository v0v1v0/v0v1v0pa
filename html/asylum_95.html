support_received R Documentation

Asylum seekers in receipt of support

Description

Asylum seekers in receipt of support at end of period, by nationality, support type, and UK region.

Usage

support_received

Format

A data frame with 68,369 rows and 7 variables:

Date

Date

Nationality

Nationality of people receiving support

Region

World region the nation is in

Support Type

Section 95, Section 98, or Section 4 support

Accommodation Type

'Dispersed Accommodation' or 'Subsistence Only' for Section 95 support

UK Region

Where the people are currently living

People

Number of people receiving support

...

Source

https://www.gov.uk/government/statistical-data-sets/asylum-and-resettlement-datasets
