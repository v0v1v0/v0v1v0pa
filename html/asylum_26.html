Grant rates at initial decision (annual)

Description

Grant rate is the proportion of initial decisions which resulted in a grant of protection or other leave.

Usage

grant_rates_initial_annual

Format

A data frame with 3,312 rows and 6 variables:

Year

Nationality

Grant

Total number of grants at initial decision

Refused

Total number of refusals at initial decision

Initial grant rate
