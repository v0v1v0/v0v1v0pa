RICENFWater R Documentation

Rice Cultivation under Natural Floods water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Rice Cultivation under Natural Floods.

Format

A data frame with 7 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WmAv1 - Mean precipitation of first month (mm)

  • WmAv2 - Mean precipitation of second month (mm)

  • WmAv3 - Mean precipitation of third month (mm)

  • WmAv4 - Mean precipitation of fourth month (mm)

  • WmhAv2 - Relative humidity of devel. Stage (%)

  • WmhAv4 - Relative humidity at harvest stage (%)

  • WynN - Mean annual n/N
