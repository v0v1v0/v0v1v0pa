ONIONWater R Documentation

Onion water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Onion.

Format

A data frame with 2 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WgAv - Precipitation of growing cycle (mm)

  • TmAvDlen3 - Daylength (h) during yield form. Period
