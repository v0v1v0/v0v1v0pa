Asylum work in progress

Description

Asylum work in progress.

Usage

asylum_work_in_progress

Format

A data frame with 13 rows and 17 variables:

Date

Date

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Total Work In Progress

Number of cases that are not concluded at the given point in time

Awaiting Initial Asylum Decision

Number waiting an initial decision

Post Decision

The 'Post Decision' sub-category includes cases that have had a decision but have not appealed, are not appeal rights exhausted, and have not been removed

Asylum Appeal Outstanding

Number with appeals outstanding

Subject to Removal Action

Number of cases in the work in progress caseload have been processed through the initial stages of the asylum operations system and are now 'subject to removal action'

On Hold

'On Hold' sub-category includes pre- and post-decision absconders

Further Leave Application Outstanding

Further Leave applications from former Unaccompanied Asylum Seeking Children given Discretionary Leave

Case Age: 0:12 Months

Age of the case

Case Age: 12:24 Months

Age of the case

Case Age: 24:36 Months

Age of the case

Case Age: 36+ Months

Age of the case

Gender: Male

Number of males

Gender: Female

Number of females

Gender: Unknown

Number of people whose gender/sex is unknown
