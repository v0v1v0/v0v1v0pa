who_hc_for_age_boys R Documentation

WHO Head circumference-for-age for boys

Description

WHO Head circumference-for-age charts for boys from day 0 (birth) to day 1856

Usage

who_hc_for_age_boys

Format

A data frame with 1857 rows and 4 variables:

Day

integer Age in days

L

double Box-Cox transformation for normality

M

double Median

S

double Coefficient of variation

Source

https://www.who.int/toolkits/child-growth-standards/standards/head-circumference-for-age
