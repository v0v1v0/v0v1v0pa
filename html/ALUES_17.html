BANANAWater R Documentation

Banana water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Banana.

Format

A data frame with 2 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WyAv - Annual precipitation (mm)

  • WmDryLen - Length dry season (months : P < 1/2 PET)

See Also
