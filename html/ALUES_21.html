BARLEYWater R Documentation

Barley water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Barley.

Format

A data frame with 3 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WgAv - Precipitation of growing cycle (mm)

  • WmAv2 - Mean precipitation of second month (mm)

  • WmAv3 - Mean precipitation of third month (mm)
