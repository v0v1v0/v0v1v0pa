Modifications

New columns added to the data for Year, Month, Day, Hour and Minute. Columns representing GMT offset removed. The column date transformed to a 'POSIXct' type. Furthermore, note that: The system data in this report is is based on average 15-minute SCADA readings (MW). All figures represent net exported energy. Maximum/Minimum figures in the different jurisdictions do not necessarily occur at the same time. The maximum SNSP limit allowed on the system is 65 SNSP may exceed this maximum limit due to wind variations.
