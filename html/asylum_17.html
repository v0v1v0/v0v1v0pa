decisions_resettlement R Documentation

Initial decisions and resettlement

Description

Outcomes of asylum applications at initial decision, and refugees resettled in the UK, by nationality, age, sex, applicant type, and UASC (unaccompanied asylum-seeking children).

Usage

decisions_resettlement

Format

A data frame with 109,265 rows and 14 variables:

Date

Date

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Nationality

Nationality of people applying

Region

World region the nation is in

Case type

'Asylum Case' or 'Resettlement Case'

Case outcome group

'Grant of Other Leave', 'Grant of Protection', 'Refused', or 'Withdrawn'

Case outcome

Detailed description of the case outcome

Age

Age group of applicants

Sex

Gender/sex of applicants

Applicant type

'Main applicant' or 'Dependant'

UASC

Are they an unaccompanied asylum-seeking child?

Host Country

Host country, in cases of resettlement

Decisions

Number of decisions

...

Source

https://www.gov.uk/government/statistical-data-sets/asylum-and-resettlement-datasets
