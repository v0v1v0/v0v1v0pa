Lifetime Reproductive Success in House Sparrows

Description

A cross table of lifetime reproductive success (LifetimeRS) in female and male house sparrows Passer domesticus in Norway.

Format

A data frame with 9 observations on the following 3 variables.

lifetimeRS

a numeric vector

females

a numeric vector

males

a numeric vector

Source

Jensen, H., B.-E. Saether, T.H. Ringsby, J. Tufto, S.C. Griffith, and H. Ellegren. 2004. Lifetime reproductive success in relation to morphology in the House Sparrow Passer domesticus. Journal of Animal Ecology 73: 599-611.
