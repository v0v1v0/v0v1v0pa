SUGARCANEWater R Documentation

Sugar Cane water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Sugar Cane.

Format

A data frame with 4 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • Wd10 - 10 days of rainfall (mm)

  • SunH - Sunshine : hours/year

  • WynN - Mean annual n/N

  • WmhAv3 - Relative humidity of maturation Stage (%)
