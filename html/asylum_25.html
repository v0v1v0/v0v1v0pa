family_reunion R Documentation

Family reunion visa grants

Description

Family reunion visas granted to family members of refugees, by nationality, sex and age.

Usage

family_reunion

Format

A data frame with 8,376 rows and 8 variables:

Date

The period in which the Family Reunion visa was granted

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Nationality

Nationality of the visa recipient

Region

World region of the country of nationality for the visa recipient

Sex

The sex of the individual

Age

Age of individual at time of visa grant

Visas granted

The number of family reunion visas granted

...

Source

https://www.gov.uk/government/statistical-data-sets/asylum-and-resettlement-datasets
