SESAMETemp R Documentation

Sesame temp requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the temp characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Sesame.

Format

A data frame with 3 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • TgAv - Mean temperature of the growing cycle (°C)

  • TgMaxAv - Mean max temp. of growing cycle (°C)

  • TgMinAv - Mean min. temp. of growing cycle (°C)
