COFFEEROWater R Documentation

Robusta Coffee water requirement for land evaluation

Description

A dataset containing the water characteristics of the crop requirements for farming Robusta Coffee.

Format

A data frame with 4 rows and 8 columns

Details

The following are the factors for evaluation:

  • WyAv - Annual precipitation (mm)

  • WmDryLen - Length dry season (months : P < 1/2 PET)

  • WmhDryXm - Mean rel. humidity dryest month (%)

  • WmnN5 - n/N of the 5 dryest months

See Also
