Usage

age_disputes

Format

A data frame with 3,815 rows and 8 variables:

Date

Date

Year

Year

Quarter

Quarter

Nationality

Nationality of people applying

Region

Raised or resolved

'Raised' or 'Resolved'

Raised type / Resolved outcome

'Existing asylum application', 'Asylum application raised in quarter', 'Less than 18', or '18+'
